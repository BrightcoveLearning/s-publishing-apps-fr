Apple TV & iOS

  • Store details: https://itunesconnect.apple.com/login (requires Apple Developer Program - Membership, $99/year)
  • Developer Program Overview:
    • Introduction: https://developer.apple.com/programs/
    • To enroll: https://developer.apple.com/programs/enroll/
    • Info about enrollment: https://developer.apple.com/support/enrollment/
  • Suggested lead time for approval: One week
Note that it can take up to 24 hours after publishing for your app to be available on the App Store. See: AppStore - App status is ready for sale, but not in app store

Required information for Apple TV & iOS

  • App Name
  • Primary Language
  • Bundle ID (for instance: com. companyname . productname )
  • SKU (for instance:[appname] applettv)
  • Subtitle - A summary of your app that will appear under your appâ€™s name on the App Store for customers with devices running tvOS 11 or later Optional field

  • App price
  • Territory availability (Countries where you want to release the app)
  • See the Screenshot specifications document from Apple's site for requirements on app previews and screenshots
  • Promotional Text (up to 170 characters)
  • Keywords
  • Description (up to 4000 characters)
  • Support URL
  • Marketing URL (optional)
  • Version
  • Rating
  • Copyright: The name of the person or entity that owns the exclusive rights to your app, preceded by the year the rights were obtained (for example, 2008 Acme Inc. ). Do not provide a URL.
  • Representative Contact information

    • State

    • Email
  • App Review Information
