Required information

To be able to install the Brightcove {{ site.product_short }} app in your Apple device, you will need:

  • A public link or invitation email provided by Brightcove.
  • TestFlight app installed in your Apple device.

Apple Supported platforms:

  • iOS apps: iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch running iOS 8 or later.
  • tvOS apps: Apple TV running tvOS 12 or later.

A high-level view of the process is:

  • Install the TestFlight app on your device.
  • Open the public link or invitation email sent to you by Brightcove.
  • Redeem the installation code for the Brightcove {{ site.product_short }} app in the TestFlight application.
  • Run the app.

Installing the Brightcove {{ site.product_short }} iOS App

In this section, you will learn how to download the Brightcove {{ site.product_short }} iOS app to test on your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch.

  5. Click the Redeem button in the upper right corner.
  6. Paste the Brightcove redeem code.
  7. Click the Redeem button.

Installing the Brightcove {{ site.product_short }} tvOS App

In this section, you will learn how to download the Brightcove {{ site.product_short }} tvOS app to test on your Apple TV.

  1. Install TestFlight on your Apple TV.
  2. Open your invitation email on a mobile device or computer.
  3. You will receive a redeem code in the invitation email. Be sure to have the code visible so you can enter it later.
  4. Open the TestFlight app on your Apple TV.
  5. Click in the Redeem tab and enter the Brightcove redemption code.
  6. The Brightcove {{ site.product_short }} app will be installed on your device to test.
