Introduction

Brightcove needs to have a user added to your Roku account. Contact Brightcove's OTT Delivery Team for an email address to use.

Instructions

Perform the following steps to add a user address to your Roku account:

  1. Log in to your Roku Developer account.
  2. Click the user icon then My account.
  3. Click Manage User Access.
  4. Click Add a user.
  5. Complete the form:
    • Enter the email address specified by Brightcove's OTT Delivery Team.
    • Add the Role of Administrator.
    • Enter Brightcove for the Organization.
  6. Click Create.
